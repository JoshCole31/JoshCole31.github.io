Deamscapes

My role on this project was team lead. we were given a donated kiosk and were tasked with making it into something new. we walked through the space and picked a room we liked there was already a screen with element of the story in the room. But the feedback the sponsors received from it was not positive so we decided to make a game to replace it. we had to keep in mind that we would have all sorts of users playing our game some that would start it and then walk away and some that would play it all the way through so we had to make sure it looped and reset so it could be a positive experience for each new user.
